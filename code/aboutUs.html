Why choose a custom website over
templates? (e.g. Shopify, SquareSpace, etc.)

Simple put, when you're buying a suit would you rather have the general stock option,
or the suit tailored specifically for you? A custom website tailors to specific business
needs, grows as your business grows and can provide more functionality than the
average template. Template sites can't reflect what your business truly is because it
hasn't been designed around it.

Is it proven that custom sites can generate
more profit?

It has been shown statistically over and over again, that custom web designs reduce
bounce back rates, have a strong ROI. ESPN increased their revenue by 35%
through listening to customer feedback and improving their website to enable
scrolling. Or Time, who reduced bounce back rates by 15%, keeping users more
engaged on their web page through UX.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on your project. On average most basic projects take roughly 10 days to
complete. In those days we go through the design process and coding of the website.
Times vary on the website needs, amount of pages, etc. To get specific times, reach out
to us with details about your website and we will give you an estimated time of
completion.
